About

Our hotel uniquely decorates its 8-story building by blending the spirit of Çeşme with Swiss style, offering accommodation with 248 spacious guest rooms and 18 suites. Our restaurants serve with three different cuisines and include options of three unique bars for your comfort. At Pürovel Spa & Sport, you will feel reborn, taking a fresh breath, while enjoying getting in shape at our adjacent fitness center.


Our hotel is ideally located at just 5.7 km from Çeşme Center, 79.8 km from İzmir city center, and 86.5 km from İzmir Adnan Menderes International Airport. Upon landing at the airport, you can inquire about our private transfer service by contacting our hotel, besides the taxi option, and enjoy a hassle-free and luxurious journey to our hotel.
